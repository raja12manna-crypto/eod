How to play

Classic Klondike solitaire, played with the Elements of Design deck. Every card is a real design term — flip the deck, learn the craft.

  • Four suits = four design disciplines: Color Theory, Branding, Typography, Design Terminology.
  • Goal: build each foundation (top right) from A → K in a single discipline.
  • Tableau: stack cards in descending order, alternating cool (green/blue) and warm (red/yellow) suits. Only a K can move to an empty column.
  • Stock: tap the deck (top left) to draw. Recycle it when empty.
  • Move cards by dragging, or tap a card then tap where it should go. Double-tap sends a card to its foundation.
  • Learn: tap the i on any face-up card to read the design term. The Terms counter tracks your progress to 52.
